HEADING

The goal of the Academy is to produce top quality players by providing excellent coaching and superior playing opportunities to elite athletes. It is vital for Academy parents to recognize that a young player has huge potential than can best be developed in an environment that emphasizes development of technique, skill and tactical play while allowing players the freedom to make mistakes and develop their own creativity. The process of developing top level players is a long term one; there are no short cuts in the creation of elite players. At our Academy you will meet dedicated and highly qualified staff who will work hard to enable your child to be successful.

At the Academy we see each player as unique, with individual needs and talents. It is our job to provide players with a safe and nurturing environment in which they can grow and develop as players and people. Involvement in soccer for children is a lifelong opportunity to make new friends, have a healthy lifestyle and to be part of a team throughout a balanced life. Developing respect, character and attitude in young athletes is the foundation to developing high quality players. Great emphasis is put on fair play and encouraging a work ethic and good habits in Academy players. We appreciate the opportunity that you, as parents, give us to build and prepare your child for their life ahead.
